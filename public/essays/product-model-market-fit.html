Beyond PMF: The Era of Product-Model-Market Fit (PMMF)

A brief case study of Manus, and a framework for why some AI products fit and others quietly fall apart.

Product-market fit was built for a world where the product is the fixed thing and the market is the variable you chase. AI breaks that assumption. The model underneath you keeps changing, and a product that fits perfectly today can be obsolete, or trivially cloned, a model release later.

In this piece I argue for a third axis, Product-Model-Market Fit, and use Manus as a case study to show what it looks like when product, model, and market actually line up, and what happens when one of them drifts out from under you.
