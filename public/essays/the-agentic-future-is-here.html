The Agentic Future Is Here: What I Predicted in 2024, and What Actually Happened

In the summer of 2024, just after I pivoted from macro to AI, I wrote down what I thought agents would become. This goes back and grades those predictions against what actually happened.

In mid-2024, fresh off a markets desk and newly focused on AI applications, I posted a set of predictions about where agents were headed. Some were early, a few were plainly wrong, and a handful held up better than I had any right to expect.

This piece walks back through them honestly: what I called right, what I missed, and what the gap between the two taught me about reading a field that moves this fast. I think grading your own forecasts out loud is the only real way to get better at making them, so I do it here in public.
